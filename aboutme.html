About me

Hello, my name is Annete and I’m from Estonia. I’m a tattoo artist and I’ve been tattooing for 6 years.

I can do almost every tattoo style you can imagine, like: fantasy, dotwork, minimalism, abstract, blackwork, neo traditional, fine line, realism, japanese, etc.

I've been practicing art my whole life and I really love my job!
